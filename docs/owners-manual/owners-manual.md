## First Use (Setup)

Follow these steps the first time you power on DawnDeck.

### 1) Power on
1. Plug in the DawnDeck power.
2. Wait for it to boot.

### 2) Connect to Wi‑Fi (only if not configured yet)
If DawnDeck has **no Wi‑Fi network configured**, the first screen you see will be **Available WiFi Networks** (instead of the clock).

1. Tap your Wi‑Fi network name.
2. Enter the password using the on-screen keyboard.
  - Tap **SHIFT** for caps.
  - Use the backspace button next to the password field to delete.
3. Tap **Connect**.
4. When it succeeds, DawnDeck will return home automatically.

### 3) Set your timezone (recommended)
1. From the clock, tap to open the radial menu.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Tap **Timezone**.
4. Tap your region/city.
  - The list shows a live “current time” preview for each zone.

### 4) Set an alarm (and confirm it’s enabled)
1. From the clock, tap to open the radial menu.
2. Tap **Alarm** once to turn it **On**.
  - You should see a bell icon + the alarm time on the clock screen.
3. Press-and-hold **Alarm** to open the alarm setup page.
4. Scroll the hour and minute selectors to set your wake time.

### 5) Pick an alarm sound and set volume
1. Open **Alarm** (press-and-hold from the clock radial menu).
2. Tap **Alarm Sound** and choose a sound.
3. Open **Media Player** and drag the **volume bar** to set a comfortable alarm volume.

### 6) (Optional) Configure and test Sunrise
1. Open **Alarm** (press-and-hold from the clock radial menu).
2. Tap **Sunrise**.
3. Set **Duration** and **Brightness**.
4. Tap **Start Sunrise**.
5. Tap/click the screen to stop the sunrise.

## The Basics (Navigation)

### Home screen (Clock)
1. The clock is the home screen.
2. When an alarm is enabled, a bell icon and the alarm time appear near the bottom.

### Radial menu (the ring menu)
1. From the clock screen, tap anywhere to open the radial menu.
2. Tap a menu item for its quick action.
3. Press-and-hold (~0.5s) a menu item to open that feature’s full page.

### Going back
On most pages, you can swipe right from the left side of the screen to go back.

## Alarm

### Turn the alarm on/off (quick toggle)
1. From the clock screen, open the radial menu.
2. Tap **Alarm** to toggle it **On/Off**.

### Set the alarm time
1. From the clock screen, open the radial menu.
2. Press-and-hold **Alarm** to open the alarm setup page.
3. Scroll the **hour** and **minute** selectors to your desired wake time.
   - In **12h** mode, AM/PM is shown; it toggles as you scroll across 11 ↔ 12.

### Choose the alarm sound
You can set the alarm sound in two ways:

**A) From the Alarm page**
1. Open **Alarm** (press-and-hold from the clock radial menu).
2. Select **Alarm Sound**.
3. If you already picked an alarm sound, you’ll go to the player for that sound.
   - Otherwise you’ll be taken to sound categories to choose one.

**B) From the Media Player (“Set Alarm” button)**
1. Play a sound (see “Sounds” below).
2. In the Media Player, tap the **Set Alarm** (bell) control to set/unset the currently loaded sound as the alarm sound.

### Set alarm volume (the volume used for wake-up)
1. Load/play your desired **alarm sound**.
2. In **Media Player**, drag the **volume bar** to set your preferred level.
   - While the alarm sound is the active sound, adjusting volume updates the saved alarm volume.

## Sunrise

The sunrise feature controls the lighting sequence and (optionally) fades in the alarm sound from silence up to your saved alarm volume.

### Configure sunrise
1. Open **Alarm** (press-and-hold from the clock radial menu).
2. Select **Sunrise** to open **Sunrise Settings**.
3. Adjust:
   - **Duration** (how long the sunrise runs)
   - **Brightness** (sunrise peak brightness)

### Start a sunrise now (test)
1. Go to **Sunrise Settings**.
2. Select **Start Sunrise**.
3. To stop the sunrise, tap/click the screen.

### Advanced: Customize sunrise steps (presets)

DawnDeck builds the sunrise sequence from a set of built-in **light presets**:

Night → First Light → Dawn → Sunrise → Day

Each preset contains a lamp + projector color mix and a brightness level. Editing these presets changes the “steps” the sunrise uses.

#### Edit presets
1. From the clock, tap to open the radial menu.
2. Press-and-hold **Lights** to open **Ambience Control**.
3. Tap the **Favorites** (list) control.
4. Select one of the preset steps (Night, First Light, Dawn, Sunrise, Day).
5. Adjust the look:
  - Drag the lamp/projector color controls to change the mix.
  - Use the brightness controls to change intensity.
6. Your changes are saved automatically. Repeat for the other preset steps.

## Sounds (Built-in sounds + Bluetooth audio)

### Quick play/pause from the clock
1. From the clock screen, open the radial menu.
2. Tap **Sounds**:
   - If an in-app sound is playing, this toggles play/pause.
   - If Bluetooth audio is active, this sends play/pause to the Bluetooth source.
3. Press-and-hold **Sounds** to open the full **Media Player**.

### Browse and play built-in sounds
1. Go to **Sound Categories**.
2. Select a category (or **Favorites**).
3. Select a sound to start playback and open **Media Player**.

### Media Player controls
In **Media Player**:
- **Volume bar**: drag left/right to change system volume.
- Radial controls:
  - **List**: back to sound categories
  - **Prev / Play-Pause / Next**: navigate playback
  - **Repeat**: loops the current in-app sound (when using built-in sounds)

### Favorite a sound
1. Start playing a built-in sound.
2. In **Media Player**, tap the **Favorite** (heart) control.
3. Access favorites from **Sound Categories → Favorites**.

### Bluetooth pairing (for phone/tablet audio)
1. Open **Settings → Bluetooth → Add Device**.
2. Pairing starts automatically; the screen shows “ready to pair…”
3. On your phone/tablet, open Bluetooth settings and look for **DawnDeck**.
4. Select it to pair.
5. Once paired, start music on your phone and open **Media Player** to see track/artist and control playback.

## Lights (Lamp + Projector ambience)

### Quick toggle (clock shortcut)
1. From the clock screen, open the radial menu.
2. Tap **Lights** to toggle **both** the lamp and projector active state.
3. Press-and-hold **Lights** to open the full ambience controls.

### Ambience control (color + brightness)
In **Ambience Control** you can adjust **projector** (top half) and **lamp** (bottom half):
- Drag within each light control to mix colors.
- Use the small **brightness** controls to raise/lower brightness.

### Save/recall ambience favorites
- **Add to Favorites**: saves the current lamp/projector look.
- **Favorites**: opens the favorites list.
- To remove a saved favorite while viewing it, tap **Add to Favorites** again (it toggles off).

## Settings

Open from the clock radial menu (press-and-hold not required).

### Screen brightness
- **Settings → Screen Brightness**
- Adjust to match your room (dimmer at night helps reduce glare).

### UI color
- **Settings → UI Color**
- Drag around the color ring to pick the accent color used throughout the interface.

### Time format (12h/24h)
- **Settings → Time Format** toggles between 12h and 24h.

### Timezone
- **Settings → Timezone**
- Select your timezone; the list shows a live preview of the current time in each zone.

### Colon blink
- **Settings → Colon Blink** toggles whether the clock colon blinks.

### Wi‑Fi
1. **Settings → Wi‑Fi**
2. Select a network.
3. Enter the password using the on-screen keyboard:
   - Use **SHIFT** for caps.
   - Use the backspace button next to the password field to delete.
4. Tap **Connect**.
5. On success, the device shows “Connected successfully!” and returns home automatically.
